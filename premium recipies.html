:GARLICK BREADSTICKS:
_COOK IN 12MIN_

  1. INGREDIENTS: A1cup all purpose flour_1/2 teaspoon yeast_1/4 teaspoon salt_1/3 cup warm water_2 tablespoon butter,melted_2 cloves garlic,minced_ 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  2. INSTRUNCTIONS: APreheat oven to 200^C(400^f)_Mix flour, yeast,salt and warm water to form dough. Let rest % minutes._Roll dough into sticks,place on baking sheet._mix butter, garlic and oregano_brush over sticks_Bake 10-12  minute until golden. serve warm now.
  3. TIPS:ASprinkle grated cheese before baking for cheesy garlic bread_serve with pasta or soup

:CHOCOLATE BROWNIES:
_COOK IN 25MIN_

  1. INGREDIENTS: 1/2 cup butter-melted,_ 1 cup sugar, 2 large eggs_,1 teaspoon vanilla extra,_1/3 cup cocoa powder,_1/2 cup all purpose Flour,_ 1/4 teaspoon salt_ 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  2. INSTRUNCTIONS: Preheat oven to 175^C(350^)_Grease a baking pan._Mix melted butter and sugar _Add eggs and vanilla stir untill smooth_Sift in coco,floor,salt and baking powder, Mix gently_Pour batter into pan and bake 20-25 minutes. check with a toothpick_Now try it
  3. TIPS:Add walnut or chocolate chips for extra texture_Don,t overbake it must be soft inside.                                                                                                         VIDEO LINK: 

:MANGO PUDDING:
_COOK IN 5MIN_

  1. INGREDIENTS: 1 CUP  mango puree_ 1 cup milk_2 tablespoon sugar_1 tablespoon gelatin powder_Fresh mango slices.
  2. Instrnctions: ASoften gelatin in 2 tablespoon water_Heat milk with sugar until warm_Add gelatin and stir until dissolved_Mix in Mango puree_Pour into serving cups_Chill in refregerator 2-3 hours untill set_ Garnish with Mango slices before serving.
  3. TIPS: AUse ripe mangoes for  natural swetness_Can add a few drops of vanilla for extra flavour

:Chow Mein:

  1. INGREDIENTS: Egg noodles, chicken or beef, cabbage, carrots, soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, garlic.
  2. INSTRUNCTIONS.  Cook noodles → stir-fry meat & veggies → add noodles + sauces → toss well.
  3. 👉 Tip: Cook noodles slightly underdone so they don’t get mushy when stir-fried.
