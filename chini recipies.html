:Sichuan Spicy Noodles:

  1. Ingredients: Wheat noodles, chili oil, garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, Sichuan peppercorns, peanuts, green onions.
  2. INSTRUNCTIONS:  Cook noodles, drain. Mix chili oil, garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, and Sichuan peppercorns. Toss noodles in sauce. Garnish with crushed peanuts and green onions.

:Kung Pao Chicken:

  1. INGREDIENTS. Chicken breast, peanuts, dried chili peppers, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, cornstarch, green onions, sesame oil.
  2. INSTRUNCTIONS. A Marinate chicken with soy sauce and cornstarch. Stir-fry garlic, ginger, and dried chili peppers. Add chicken and cook until done. Mix soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and sesame oil, then add to the pan. Stir in peanuts and green onions. Serve hot.

:Sweet and Sour Pork:

  1. INGREDIENTS:  Pork, bell peppers, pineapple, onion, vinegar, ketchup, sugar, soy sauce, cornstarch, garlic, oil.
  2. INSTRUNCTIONS: Coat pork pieces in cornstarch and fry until crispy. Stir-fry garlic, peppers, and onion. Add pineapple and sauce made from vinegar, ketchup, sugar, and soy sauce. Mix pork back in and cook until sauce thickense .
